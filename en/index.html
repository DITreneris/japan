Foundation
~3–5 min

AI Context Check

Check what ChatGPT knows about your organization and where it might go wrong

META: You are a critical business analyst. Goal: find out what the AI actually knows about the organization and where clarification is needed.

INPUT: Company [COMPANY]. Based only on publicly available information and general market knowledge, describe how YOU understand this company. Structure: 1) What you know for certain 2) What assumptions you make 3) Where information is missing and you might be wrong. Clearly mark at the end: "These points need clarification from the user."

OUTPUT: Result: a short structured answer – you can clearly see where the AI might be wrong and what to clarify before going further.

Before using

Use when: you start analyzing an organization; you want to check what the AI knows about the company; you’re preparing to use AI with context.

Replace before using:

  • [COMPANY] → your or your client’s company (e.g. Acme Inc).

What to do: This is not a questionnaire. Copy the text below and paste it into ChatGPT or Claude.

Why it matters

Reduces AI “hallucinations” and wrong decisions – you see clearly what the AI actually knows about your context.

Organization Portrait

Build a clear profile and context of your organization

INPUT: Company [COMPANY]. Analyze and describe: approximate size (employees, revenue); main business areas; management/organizational model; typical challenges in this sector. Keep the answer short and structured. Mark where you are making assumptions.

My Role in the Organization

Clearly define your role’s purpose, responsibilities and impact

META: You are an organization design consultant. Goal: describe the user’s role in the organization – purpose, responsibilities and impact.

INPUT: Role [MY ROLE], company [COMPANY]. Based on the organization analysis, describe this role. Include: the role’s main purpose; 5–7 core responsibilities; decision level (operational / tactical / strategic); who they report to and who they work with daily. Write concisely, no theory.

OUTPUT: Result: a clear role description – use as a reference for the next steps.

Before using

Use when: you want to clarify your role; you’re starting with a new company; you need a reference for your position.

Replace before using:

  • [COMPANY] → your or your client’s company (e.g. Acme Inc);

Core Work Processes

Identify where time and energy go (Pareto 80/20)

META: You are a business process analyst. Goal: identify the 5 most important work processes (Pareto 80/20) – where time and energy go.

INPUT: Role [MY ROLE], company [COMPANY]. Describe the 5 most important processes this role actually carries out. For each: purpose; main steps; roles involved; where it usually gets stuck. 80% actions, 20% explanation.

OUTPUT: Result: a list of processes so you can see where AI will have the biggest impact (step 6).

AI Help and Optimization

Turn AI into a real work assistant – concrete ideas based on your processes

META: You are an AI implementation consultant. Goal: assess the processes and suggest concrete ways to improve them using ChatGPT or other AI tools.

INPUT: Assess the (already described) processes and give 8–10 ideas. For each: what the AI does; which part of the process it eases; real benefit (time / quality / cost); implementation difficulty (easy / medium / hard). Write for a non-technical user.

OUTPUT: Result: 8–10 concrete ideas where AI can save hours per week.

Before using

Use when: you already have process descriptions (step 5); you want concrete ways to use AI; you want to save time.
